## What is YBH Pulse Content?

Pulse Content eliminates fragmented workflows by consolidating the entire content production process into a single platform:

- **Upload transcripts** edited from Adobe Podcast
- **Auto-generate** PRF documents and viral hooks
- **Create social posts** for LinkedIn, Instagram, and other platforms
- **Generate visual assets** including infographics, quote cards, and thumbnails
- **Edit everything inline** with rich text editing before export
- **Share brand kits** with guests via secure links

<Note>
  **Transcript is the single source of truth.** All content generation includes fact-checking against the original transcript to prevent hallucinations and ensure accuracy.
</Note>

## Key Features

### Content Generation Pipeline

Automatic generation workflow triggered by transcript upload:

1. **PRF (Podcast Repurposing Framework)** - Comprehensive episode analysis
2. **Viral Hooks** - Attention-grabbing soundbites for social media
3. **Episode Metadata** - Titles, descriptions, key takeaways, and show notes
4. **Social Posts** - Platform-specific content for LinkedIn and Instagram
5. **Video Clips** - Short-form clip suggestions with timestamps
6. **Visual Assets** - AI-designed infographics, quote cards, and thumbnails

### Design Studio

Create professional visual assets with AI assistance:

- **Parallel Suggestions** - Generate 4 data visualizations + 4 cinematic infographics + 2 quote cards simultaneously
- **Smart Spec Generation** - AI proposes layouts, templates, color systems, and prompts
- **Variety Tracking** - Ensures visual diversity across episodes by analyzing generation history
- **Image Generation** - Powered by Kie.ai Nano Banana Pro for high-quality renders
- **Logo Compositing** - Add YBH branding to assets before brand kit approval

### Brand Consistency

<Info>
  All generated content follows **YBH Brand Guidelines**:
  - Voice: "We don't sell. We unsell." - Anti-spin, pro-IT leader
  - Colors: Selective Yellow (#F7B500), Crusta Orange (#F17529), Sunset Orange Red (#EF4136)
  - Typography: Fonseca (headlines), Montserrat (body)
  - Target audience: IT leaders (CIOs, CTOs, IT Directors)
</Info>

### Approval Workflows

Content review and approval system:

- **PRF Approval** - Mark PRF as approved before generating downstream content
- **Hooks Approval** - Approve viral hooks for use in social posts
- **LinkedIn Approval** - Approve posts before scheduling or sharing
- **Brand Kit Curation** - Select and approve assets for guest delivery
- **Spell Check** - AI-powered grammar and spelling correction
- **Fact Check** - Validate content accuracy against transcript

## Authentication

Pulse Content uses secure email/password authentication restricted to `@popularit.net` accounts:

- **JWT-based sessions** with 24-hour expiration
- **Role-based access control** (Admin, Designer, User)
- **Rate limiting** on login attempts (5 attempts per 15 minutes)
- **Password reset** via email with Resend API
- **Public share links** for guest brand kit pages

<Warning>
  All team members have equal access to episodes and content. Guest brand kit pages (`/share/:token`) remain public and don't require authentication.
</Warning>
